Shipping & Returns

Main Shipping Terms

Free expedited shipping on orders of $200 CAD or more (before taxes and shipping) in Canada.
Orders below $199 are subject to a $4.99 shipping fee.
Free shipping in the United States for orders over $200 CAD. US Orders below $199 CAD are subject to a $9.99 shipping fee.

Returns

We want you to love what you ordered, but if something isn't right, let us know. We'll give you a refund or send you another color, a new size or an entirely different style.

You can return items by mail or at the appropriate brand's store (Canadian locations only) within 45 days of your order date.There are no returns or exchanges on final sale merchandise, either online or in stores.
