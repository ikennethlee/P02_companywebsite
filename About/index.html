About Us

Company Profile

Ascend is an outdoor clothing and sporting goods company founded in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, in 2001. The name and logo of Ascend shows rise and the direction of an arrow pointing upwards. Ascend sells outdoor clothing and bag products from two American outdoor product company: Arcteryx and The North Face. Ascend features products that ranges from outerwear, fleece, coats, shirts, pants, shorts as well as bag products. For further information, please contact our corporate office.
